How we work

We start with your business, not our solution

Before we propose anything, we sit down with you — in your shop, on your schedule — and listen. We want to understand what's eating your time, what decisions feel like guesswork, and what you'd do with an extra few hours a week.

From there, we design something for you specifically.

Some of the most valuable work we do isn't a packaged service at all — it's a small automation that eliminates a recurring chore, or a report that finally answers the question you've been guessing at for years.

If you don't see your problem below, reach out anyway. That's usually where the most interesting work begins

There are far more creative, lightweight ways to leverage technology to save time than just creating and managing a website. The best solutions are often invisible — running quietly in the background while you focus on what you love

Offerings

Where most clients start

Three focused offerings built around the patterns we see most often. Pick one, or reach out and we'll figure out what fits.

⭑ Flagship

Your Business at a Glance

Sales & Performance Reporting

Starting at Contact for pricing

We take the data sitting inside your POS (Square, Toast, Clover, etc.) and turn it into a clean, plain-English reporting workbook that answers the questions you actually care about: How much am I making? What am I making it on? When am I busiest?

What you get

  • Revenue by day, week, and month — with trends visible at a glance
  • Best and slowest-selling items and categories
  • Busiest and slowest days and dayparts, so staffing and specials can follow the money
  • A margin view showing which items make money vs. just move volume
  • One interconnected workbook you can refresh yourself with a new export

Who it's for

Any owner-operated business already on a modern POS who has never had a clear picture of their own numbers. You have the data — you just haven't had the time or the analyst to make sense of it.

Offering 2

Fix the Friction

Process Automation & Busywork Elimination

Starting at Contact for pricing

We identify the repetitive, manual tasks eating your week — menu updates, scheduling, follow-ups, reconciling numbers across systems — and build simple automations that handle them without you.

What you get

  • A documented map of your current workflow and where time is leaking
  • Targeted automations for your highest-friction tasks
  • A handoff that's fully yours — no monthly fee to Carn for it to keep running

Who it's for

Owners who feel like they're doing the same five things every week that a computer should be doing.

Offering 3

Make It Work Harder

Tech Stack Cleanup & Quick Wins

Starting at Contact for pricing

A focused audit and upgrade of the tools you already have — your website, your POS setup, your booking or ordering flow. Most small businesses set these up once and never revisited them. We find where they're leaking time or customers and fix it.

What you get

  • A review of your current tools and configuration
  • A prioritized list of quick wins, ranked by impact vs. effort
  • Implementation of the top 2–3 fixes

Who it's for

Businesses that feel like their tech is "fine" but sense there's more to squeeze from what they already pay for.

Beyond the packages
