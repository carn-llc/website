About

The business behind the business.

Why Carn exists, what we stand for, and who's doing the work.

The mission

Help Local Businesses Go Even Further.

Most small businesses are doing just fine with the tech they have — a POS, a website, an Instagram account. The problem isn't that the tools are wrong. It's that nobody has had the time to make them work harder.

Carn exists to fix that. We sit with local business owners, understand the specific friction in their day-to-day, and build quiet, durable solutions that absorb the complexity so they don't have to touch it.

The goal is never to impress anyone with technology. The goal is to make it disappear.

Why Cairn?

Carn is Gaelic for the word Cairn — a navigational trail marker in the wilderness. Like a Cairn, we strive to be your guide to go even further with the technology and data you have.
